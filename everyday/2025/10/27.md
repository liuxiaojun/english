## move on to 释义：过渡到，转换话题
* Let's move on to new business. 让我们来谈谈新业务。
* We've already moved on to the sales figures, questions can wait till later. 我们已经谈到了销售数据，其他的问题一会儿再提。

## in addition 释义：另外，此外
* In addition, I'm the editor of our yearbook. 另外，我是我们年鉴的编辑。
* In addition, we've received complaints from customers as well. 另外，我们还收到了来自消费者的投诉。

## reach out to 释义：接触
* We must reach out to those in need. 我们应该帮助那些需要帮助的人。
* Online learning helps us reach out to new friends. 网络学习使我们接触到新朋友。

## go around the table 释义：轮流，一个一个
* We went around the table and heard everyone's ideas. 让我们轮流表达，听听大家的主意。
* Dennis started going around the table asking everyone who they were voting for. 丹尼斯围着桌子一个一个地问他们要投票给谁。

## get somebody's input  释义：让某人发表意见
* Let's get her input on this before we make the final decision. 在做最后的决定前，我们先听听她的意见。
* I'd want to get everyone's input before we make the call. 在我们盖棺定论之前我想听听大家的意见。

## in accordance with 释义：按照，与……一致
* I believe this is all in accordance with state law. 我相信这都与州法律一致。
* Then the project will proceed in accordance with the decision made today. 这个项目将按照今天的决定进行。

## lose ground 释义：处于不利的位置
* Our vendors have been losing ground to chain stores. 我们的柜台已经在连锁店里处于不利的位置了。
* We lost ground all last year to our competitors. 和我们的竞争对手相比，去年我们一直处于不利的位置。

## figure out  释义：解决，想出
* It is something you should figure out yourself. 这件事情你必须得自己解决。
* I haven't figured out how we'll do this yet. 我还没有想出我们应该如何处理这件事儿。
