## run short on time 释义：时间不够
* We’re going to run short on time if we don’t hurry up. 如果我们再不抓紧，我们时间就不够了。
* Since we ran short on time, we weren’t able to cover the entire presentation. 由于时间不够，我们无法做完这个展示。

## summarize the main points 释义：总结重点，要点
* Now that I've finished the body of the presentation, I’d like to summarize the main points. 我的陈述主体就到这，让我来总结一下要点。
* It’s important to summarize the main points at the end of a meeting. 会议结束时总结重点很重要。

## get in contact with 释义：接触，与……有联系
* I’ll have my secretary get in contact with your secretary. 我会让我的秘书联系您的秘书。
* That man is very difficult to get in contact with. 那个男人很难接触。

## deepen somebody's understanding 释义：深化某人的认知，大开眼界
* We’re looking to deepen our understanding of the Beijing market. 我们想要深化我们对北京市场的了解。
* This business trip really helped me deepen my understanding of the problems I face. 这次公差使我对眼前问题的了解更加深刻了。

## Let's call it a day. 释义：到此为止
* If there’s nothing else, let’s call it a day. 如果没有别的事情，今天就到这儿吧。
* Let’s call it a day and go out for dinner. 今天就到这儿吧，我们去吃饭。

## as of 释义：自……起；到……止
* The contract takes effect as of October 1st. 该合同自十月一日起生效。
* You’ve been sitting on this email for 3 days as of today, when will you answer it? 到今天为止已经三天了，你们打算什么时候回复这邮件？

## wrap something up 释义：总结，整理
* Let’s wrap this section up and hit this problem from a new angle in the afternoon. 我们把这一部分总结一下，下午我们从一个新的角度来看这些问题。
* We were able to wrap up the deal before we went home. 我们能在回家之前整理好这次的交易。

## fruitful 释义：adj.有成果的
* This meeting has been fruitful, but we should make our meeting shorter. 这次会议很有成果，但是我们应该把会议时间缩短一点。
* This discussion wasn’t very fruitful; we didn’t even come to one decision. 这个讨论真没成果，我们甚至没有得出一个结论。
