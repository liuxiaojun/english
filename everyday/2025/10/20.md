## identify 释义：v.识别
* I can identify what type of personality a person has from a mile away. 我可以从一英里外识别一个人的性格类型。
* I've always been able to identify his handwriting because it's so sloppy. 我总是能够识别他的笔迹，因为他的字迹很潦草。

## shrink 释义：v.收缩
* We're looking to shrink the company to make it more profitable. 我们希望缩减公司支出来让公司盈利更多。
* We've already shrunk the team to just 3 people. 我们团队人数已经缩减到3人了。

## release 释义：v.发布；释放
* We can only release that information to the person whose name is on the account. 我们只能向账号名单上的人公布那个消息。
* The product release will happen next month. 产品发布会将在下个月举行。

## customer loyalty 释义：客户忠诚度
* We try to build customer loyalty by offering discount cards to customers. 我们试着给予客户折扣卡来建立客户忠诚度。
* Building a strong brand identity will help improve customer loyalty. 建立显著的品牌标志会有助于提高客户忠诚度。

## store something in a database 释义：储存……在数据库里
* The system stores the call logs in a database. 系统将通话记录存储在数据库里。
* We want to store all of our customer's purchasing information in a database. 我们想将所有客户的购买记录存储在数据库里。

## differentiate between 释义：区分
* Our search function can differentiate between relevant and irrelevant results. 我们的搜索功能可以区分相关的和不相关的结果。
* We hope HR can differentiate between good candidates and poor ones in the interview process. 我们希望人力部门可以在面试环节区分优秀候选人和较差的候选人。

## enable sb. to do sth. 释义：使某人做某事
* This will enable us to build customer loyalty over time. 久而久之这会有助于建立客户对我们的信任。
* Their cautious approach enables us to come in and take their market share. 他们谨慎的方法使我们能够进入市场并夺得他们的市场份额。
