## It's a great pleasure to 释义：做……很高兴，很荣幸做……
* It's a great pleasure to welcome our CEO to say a few words. 请大家热烈欢迎我们的CEO来讲几句。
* It's a great pleasure to work with these artists. 很荣幸和这几位艺术家共事。

## get through 释义：完成，做完
* I want to get through all of these problems before we leave. 我希望在走之前把这些问题解决了。
* We had so much to get through in the meeting that we had to skip lunch. 我们的会议需要完成很多事项，所以没有时间吃午饭了。

## get down to business 释义：言归正传, 着手做某事
* Enough talking about the weather, let's get down to business. 不聊天气了，让我们言归正传。
* It's time for us to get down to business. 是时候言归正传了。

## take the minutes 释义：会议纪要
* It’s important to have someone take the minutes in a meeting. 在会议上做会议纪要是很重要的。
* After you take the minutes, be sure to send them out to everyone who came. 记得将会议纪要发给每一个参会人员。

## over to sb. 释义：交给某人
* I'm done speaking, over to you. 我说完了，交给你了。
* Let's welcome Sally. She will give us a speech. Sally, over to you. 欢迎Sally来做一个演说。Sally,交给你了。

## refreshment 释义：n.茶点
* It's always polite to offer guests refreshments. 为客人提供茶点是非常礼貌的。
* The refreshments were served when we got to their living room. 当我们到客厅的时候茶点已经放在那了。

## address 释义：v.演说，说话
* We'll address that problem next meeting. 这个问题我们下次会议再说。
* I am going to address the sales figures next. 接下来我要讲一下销售数据。

## if time permits 释义：如果时间允许……
* We'll have a question and answer session if time permits. 如果时间允许,我们将进行问答环节。
* If time permits we'll let Amy speak. 如果时间允许,我们请Amy讲一下。
