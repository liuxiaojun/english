## become proficient at  释义：精通
* With this training session, you'll become proficient at using online office software. 通过这个培训，你会对于使用网络办公软件很熟悉。
* I'm already proficient at talking with customers. 我已经很擅长跟客户交流了。

## some specifics 释义：一些具体细节
* I'd like to see some specifics on this proposal. 我想知道这个提案的一些详细内容。
* He gave us some specifics on the trade deal. 他告诉了我一些商业交易的具体细节。

## give somebody a demo  释义：给某人演示
*  He gave us a demo on how to use the new call software. 他给我们做了一个如何使用新呼叫软件的演示。
* I had to give our customers a product demo at the sales meeting. 在销售会上我必须给我们的客户做一个产品演示。

## a great time saver 释义：省时的事情
* The new search system is a great time saver. 新的搜索系统非常省时。
* Using the new software is a great time saver when it comes to finding old records. 当查找旧记录时，使用新系统非常省时。

## register for 释义：注册
* You have to register for your employee card. 你必须注册才能得到员工卡。
* We had to register for our computers after orientation. 入职培训后，我们必须注册申请我们的个人电脑。

## conduct 释义：v.组织
* The company appointed a special investigator to conduct its own inquiry.
公司委派了一个特别调查员组织调查。

## conference call 释义：n.电话会议
* We have a weekly conference call with all of our branch offices.
我们每周会与所有的分公司开一次电话会议。
* The last conference call we did had some technical problems.
我们上次进行的电话会议有一些技术问题。

## complicated 释义：adj.复杂的
* The design for this product is too complicated for me to figure out.
我弄不明白这个产品的设计思路，因为它太复杂难懂了。
* The relationship between our two companies is very complicated.
我们两个公司的关系很复杂。
