## positive feedback 释义：正向反馈
* Have you ever had any positive feedback from the customers about the product in this month? 这个月你有收到客户对于我们产品的一些正向反馈吗？
* New graduates want to have success and need to receive positive feedback often. 刚毕业的大学生们追求成功，需要经常性的积极评价和反馈。

## freight insurance 释义：运费险
* Our company offers import and export freight insurance for customers, according to the insurance clauses. 按照客户保险条款要求, 我公司为客户办理进出口货运险业务。
* Company Delivery World offers services for air delivery and freight insurance. 配送世界公司提供航空速递，货运保险服务。

## extra charge 释义：额外收费，另收费
* You can have your meals in your room at a small extra charge. 你只要额外付点钱, 就可以在你的房间里用餐。
* In this way, you can pay for purchases a month later, without any extra charge. 这样，你可以在一个月以后再支付货款，不收任何额外的费用。

## ground transport  释义：地面运输
* The new traffic rule helps a great deal to reduce the congestion of ground transport. 新的交规很大程度上缓解了地面交通拥堵的情况。
* Fares do not include ground transport service between airports or between airports and town terminals. 票价不包括机场与机场之间或者机场与市区之间的地面运输。

## freight collect  释义：运费到付； 运费向收货人索取
* Our policy is normally to pay freight collect for our new samples. 我们的政策是通常新样品支付运费到付。
* We hereby guarantee the payment of all freight collect charges due to quality problems. 在此，由于产品质量问题，我们保证提供运费到付服务。

## carry out  释义：实施；执行
* How delighted I am to read that your cinema has got the money to carry out improvements. 当我得知你们电影院已经获得经费进行改善时，我真的非常高兴。
* The president needs a clear head to carry out her instructions. 总裁需要一个头脑清醒的人来执行自己的指令。

## be fond of 释义：喜欢
* When would you be fond of receiving the call? 你喜欢什么时候叫您？
* I am fond of singing but do not regard it as a lifelong career. 我喜欢唱歌，但我还不愿意把它当作我的终身职业。

## stock order 释义：补充仓库订单
* Our products are so popular in African markets that we have received a number of stock orders this quarter. 我们产品在非洲市场很受欢迎，所以这个季度我们已经收到了一批补充仓库的订单。
* Complete application information is required for all systems purchased unless these systems are a stock order. 如果这些采购系统并非库存订单，那么就需要完整的申请信息。
