## equipment 释义：n.设备
* Take care when using old electrical equipment. 使用旧电器设备时要小心。
* Everyone should bring the necessary equipment for camping in the mountains. 每个人都应该带上在山上露营所需的装备。

## facility 释义：n.设施
* What facilities and equipment are available? 有哪些设施和设备？
* The museum offers extensive facilities for study. 这个博物馆提供了许多供研究的设备。

## gym 释义：n.健身房
* The gym is only two blocks away from my home. 健身房离我家只有两个街区。
* Sometimes he goes to the gym to work out. 有时候他去健身房锻炼。

## company restaurant  释义：公司食堂
* Do you eat in the company restaurant? 你在公司餐厅吃饭吗？
* Eating in the company restaurant is cheaper than outside. 在公司餐厅吃饭比外面更便宜。

## office supplies 释义：办公用品
* I need to order some office supplies for the office. 我需要为办公室订购一些办公用品。
* You can find all the necessary office supplies in the supply closet. 你可以在供给柜里找到所有必要的办公用品。

## tea break 释义：茶歇
* We have a tea break at 10 am every day. 我们每天上午10点都有一个茶歇。
* During the tea break, everyone gathered to chat and relax. 在茶歇期间，每个人都聚在一起聊天、放松。

## specific 释义：a.具体的
* Let's look at some specific functions. 让我们来看一下具体的功能。
* Here are a few additional, specific notes from the meeting. 以下是会议中提出的一些具体的额外事宜。

## appreciate 释义：v.感激
* I'd appreciate some help. 如果有人帮忙，我将非常感激。
* It will be greatly appreciated if you will kindly send us your samples. 如承惠寄样品，则不胜感激。

## guidance 释义：n.指导
* Under his guidance, we finished the work smoothly. 在他的指导下, 我们顺利地完成了工作。
* This method provides useful guidance for engineering practice. 该方法对工程实践具有指导意义。
