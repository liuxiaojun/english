## clear customs 释义：通过海关检查
* Do you have any idea how long it will take to clear customs? 你知道通关要多长时间吗?
* After you clear customs, you can exchange your money. 你办完通关手续后，就可以去换钱了。

## immigration counter 释义：入境窗口
* Can you lead us to immigration counter for foreigners? 请告诉我们持外国护照在哪里出关?
* I'm told the customs officer will check the passport at the immigration counter. 我听说要在入境窗口检查护照。

## declaration form 释义：申报单
* Please have your customs declaration form ready. 请把您的申报表准备好。
* When you are approaching your destination, you'll have to fill in the customs declaration form. 抵达目的地时，你必须填写海关报税单。

## personal belongings 释义：私人物品
* You do not have to pay duty on personal belongings. 个人物品不必缴税。
* The suitcase contains all his personal belongings. 箱子里放的都是他的个人物品。

## scan one's fingerprint 释义：扫描某人指纹
* You have to scan your fingerprint when you go through customs. 过关的时候你必须要扫描你的指纹。
* Please put your finger on it to scan your fingerprint. 请将手指放在这上面扫描你的指纹。

## intend to 释义：打算要……
* I intend to spend the night here. 我打算在这过夜。
* Where do you intend to go for Christmas? 圣诞节你打算去哪?

## declare 释义：v.报关
* The customs asked me if I had anything to declare. 海关人员问我是否有要报关的东西。
* Have you anything to declare? Any wines, perfumes or silks? 你有什么东西要报关吗？有酒，有香水或是丝绸吗?

## prohibited articles  释义：禁运物品
* Shippers are also advised to refer to each country's Import Prohibited Articles list. 我们也建议寄件人参考各国的进口禁运物品清单。
* Please do not put prohibited articles in EMS. 邮件内请勿夹寄禁运物品。
