## proceed to 释义：继续做……
* I’d like to proceed to the next item on our list. 我想要继续讨论清单上的下一个项目。
* After this, we should proceed to urgent business. 做完这个任务之后，我们应该转至紧急的业务。

## over budget 释义：超过预算
* We went over budget on the special effects. 在特效上我们的花费超过预算了。
* Our vacation went over budget after we decided to stay in Paris an extra week . 决定在巴黎多呆一周后，我们的假期花费超过了预算。
