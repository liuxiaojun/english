## be addicted to 释义：对……上瘾
* I’m so addicted to Candy Crush that I play it every day 我对消消乐游戏特别上瘾，天天玩它。
* There’re a lot of people these days that are addicted to the Internet. 现在有很多人对网络特别地上瘾。

## sort things out  释义：把……捋顺
* We need to get this sorted out before we move on. 我们必须在采取下一步行动之前把这事理顺。
* He’s the man who wants to help us sort this out. 他正是那个想要帮我们把事捋清楚的人。

## invest in 释义：对……投资
* We are happy to invest in a company whose future looks so bright. 我们很乐意投资那些有前景的公司。
* I’m looking for people to invest in my new restaurant. 我正在找人投资我的新饭店。

## competitive advantage 释义：竞争优势
* We hope that our new design will give us a competitive advantage over their products. 希望我们的新的设计能让我们在同行竞品中占有优势。
* Our brand recognition is still a competitive advantage over their company. 与他们的公司相比，我们的品牌认知度还是有优势的。

## explore the market  释义：开拓市场
* We’re going to look at ways to explore the market over the next year. 我们正在想办法来开拓明年的市场。
* I’d like you to explore the market and give me a report by next week. 我希望你能开拓一下市场，并在下周前给我一份报告。

## marketing strategy 释义：市场战略
* Our marketing strategy includes plenty of ads on the Internet. 我们的市场战略囊括了很多的在线广告。
* Their marketing strategy is failing, and we shouldn’t copy it. 他们的市场战略渐渐失去优势，我们不能学习。

## phone application 释义：手机应用
* WeChat is the phone application I use the most. 微信是我用的最多的手机应用。
* I can’t bring myself to purchase a phone application. 我不能再买手机应用了。

## peak hour 释义：高峰期
* We need to book more spots on peak hours if we want to get the most viewers. 如果我们想得到尽可能多的观众的话，我们需要在高峰期预留位置。
* The peak hours for TV are normally on the weekdays right after dinnertime. 电视的高峰期通常在工作日的晚餐后。
