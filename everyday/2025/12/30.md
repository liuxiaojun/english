## So far so good. 释义：目前为止一切都好。
* In regards to our investments, so far so good. 关于我们的投资,目前为止一切都好。
* So far so good, but we're facing some difficulties coming up. 目前为止一切都好，但是接下来我们要面对一些困难。

## formal invitation 释义：邀请函
* Make sure you keep your formal invitations. They won't let you in the event without one. 请保管好您的邀请函，否则他们将不让您进入。
* They just sent out formal invitations for their wedding last week. 他们上星期刚把结婚请柬寄出。

## fair guidebook 释义：展会指导手册
* The trade fair guidebook has all the information you need for the entire event. 展会指导手册上有你需要的整个活动的信息。
* The fair guidebook is given away for free at the entrance. 入口处有免费发放的会展指导手册。

## opening ceremony 释义：开幕式
* He's going to give a short speech at the opening ceremony to the employees. 他将在开幕式上为所有的雇员做一个演说。
* The opening ceremony is going to start a little late, I'm afraid. 恐怕开幕式要晚点才能进行了。

## banner 释义：n．横幅，标语
* We've got the company name printed on a banner. 我们在横幅上印了公司的名字。
* We hung banners all over the building promoting our event. 我们在大楼上挂满了横幅来宣传我们的活动。

## brochure 释义：n．小册子
* The brochure has a listing of our products and prices. 这本小册子上列了我们的产品及价格。
* The computer's features are detailed in our brochure. 该计算机的特点在我们的小册子中有详细介绍。

## giveaway 释义：n．赠品
* With these free giveaways, we'll definitely be the most popular booth at the fair. 有了这些赠品，我们展台一定是最受欢迎的。
* Free book giveaway for all who attend. 为所有参加者准备的免费赠书。
