## Perhaps you would care to 释义：或许你乐意……
* Perhaps you would care to look at our list of fine wines before you order? 您是否需要在预定前看一下我们的美酒列表？
* Perhaps you would care to have dinner with me over the weekend? 或许你愿意周末和我一起吃晚餐？

## Let me buy you dinner 释义：让我请你吃晚餐吧
* You’ve done so much work for us. Let me buy you dinner. 你为我们做了这么多，让我请你吃晚餐吧。
* Let me buy you dinner. We can keep talking about this over some food. 让我请你吃晚餐吧。吃饭的时候咱们可以继续讨论这个问题。

## there is a place 释义：……有一个地方……
* There is a place downtown that serves authentic Italian pasta I could take you to. 我可以带你去一个市中心有正宗意大利食物的餐厅。
* There is a place in Chinatown that does dimsum just like mom used to make it. 唐人街有一家卖点心的地方，那个味道好像妈妈做的一样。

## plenty of  释义：大量的
* We had plenty of new ideas on how to solve the problem, unfortunately, none of them were workable. 我们有过非常多解决问题的想法，不幸的是，没一个行得通。
* He’s had plenty of time to think about this, we need the answer now! 已经给了他大量时间去思考这件事，我们现在就需要答案！

## How's that sound? 释义：怎么样？
* Let’s finish this tomorrow after I buy you dinner. How’s that sound? 明天我请你吃完饭后咱们再完成这项任务，怎么样？
* Let’s go get lunch at Panera’s. How’s that sound? 咱们去Panera家吃午饭吧。 怎么样？

## assume 释义：v.假设
* I assume he’s just running late and isn’t standing us up!
我估计他时间来不及了而且也不支持我们。
* He assumed they would just agree so he had already told his boss the deal was cut and dried, but when he talked to the client they said no. 他自以为对方已经同意了，所以他早已告诉他的老板这事儿已经搞定了，但是当他和客户交流的时候，客户拒绝了。

## presence 释义：n.出席，存在
* His presence has been very helpful to me over these past few weeks. 在过去的几周里，他的存在对我有很大的帮助。
* His supervisor’s presence made him work harder. 领导在他边上促使他更加努力的工作。

## spacious 释义：adj.宽敞的
* Their house was very spacious. 他们家的房间原先非常宽敞。
* The convention center had a spacious lobby that could fit 400 people. 会议中心有着一个可以容纳400人的宽敞的大堂。
