## May I take this opportunity to ?  
* May I take this opportunity to talk to you about our new product? 我可以借此契机来和你谈谈我们的新产品吗？
* May I take this opportunity to step out and make a quick personal call? 我可以借此机会出门打个电话吗？

## take a rain check 释义：改期
* I'll take a rain check on that invitation to have dinner at your home. 我改天再接受去你家吃饭的邀请。
* He took a rain check on coming and touring our offices. 他改天才会来看我们的办公室了。

## be fully booked 释义：（日程）排满了
* He was fully booked last week. That's why he didn't call you. 他上周特别忙。这就是为什么他没给你打电话
* Sorry, I'm fully booked all day Thursday. I guess I'll have to meet you next Monday then. 抱歉，我周四全天都没有时间，所以我大概只能下周一和你见面了。

## Would be acceptable?  释义：……可行吗？
* Would it be acceptable to reserve the order? 可以帮我预留订单吗？
* Would purple be acceptable? 紫色可以吗？

## Perhaps you would care to 释义：或许你乐意……
* Perhaps you would care to have a glass of ice water? 或许你想来一杯冰水？
* Perhaps you would care to stop by at a later time. 或许你想晚些时候过来。

## upcoming 释义：adj.即将来临的
* The upcoming Olympics will be held in 2016. 下一届奥运会将在2016年举行。
* Our upcoming shareholders meeting will take place next month. 下一届股东大会将会在下个月举行。

## display 释义：v.展示
* We'll be displaying all of our newest products at the next exhibition. 我们将会在下一届展销会上展示我们所有的新品。
* Will you be displaying our exhibit near the front of the convention hall? 你将会在会议大厅的前面展示我们的展品吗？

## broaden our horizons 释义：开阔视野
* I feel like the trip to India really helped us to broaden our horizons. 我觉得印度之旅确实帮助我们开阔了眼界。
* We wanted to broaden our horizons, so we went backpacking in Nepal. 我们想开阔眼界，所以我们去尼泊尔背包旅行了。
