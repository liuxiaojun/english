## be keen on  释义：喜爱
* We are very keen on your proposal. 我们非常喜欢你的提案。
* He is keen on getting finished and out of the office by 6. 他喜欢在6点之前完成工作并离开办公室。

## stand out  释义：突出；引人注目
* He stood out from his peers due to his strong work ethic. 强烈的职业道德使他特别出众。
* This quarter's sales really stood out as being different from the rest. 这一季销量与其他季度相比真的很突出。

## establish priority 释义：建立优先事项
* We need to establish priorities on which project needs to be finished first. 我们需要确定优先项目优先完成。
* From now on, we're going to establish new priorities around here. 从现在开始，我们将在此建立新的优先事项。

## handle interruption 释义：处理打断事件
* He's really bad at handling interruptions; he'll just sit there and nod while the other person talks on and on. 他不擅长打断别人，他只是在别人一直说话的时候坐在那点头。
* He handles interruptions by just directly telling the other person to shut up and wait their turn! 他处理打断的方法就是直接告诉其他人闭嘴等着。

## degree of urgency 释义：紧急度
* The degree of urgency on this matter is quite low, finish it whenever you can get around to it. 这个问题的紧急度很低，有空的时候再做就行。
* They don't quite understand the degree of urgency on this project. 他们不太明白这个项目的紧急度。

## prioritize 释义：v.按重要性排列
* You should make a list of all the tasks you have to do and prioritize them. 你应该把所有必须做的任务都列出来，并按轻重缓急排个顺序。
* We prioritized poorly and didn’t finish the project on time. 我们没有做好优先处理，没有及时完成这个项目。

## circulate 
* Did everyone get a chance to look at the handout that’s circulating? 有人看到正在传阅的资料了吗？
* You will have a chance to circulate and chat informally. 你们会有机会四处走动一下, 互相聊聊。

## restrict 释义：v.限制
* The number of people allowed on this bus is restricted to 20. 这辆汽车限乘乘客数量为20。
* This area is restricted to military personnel only!
这个区域仅限军事人员出入。
