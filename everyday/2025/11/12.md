## unknown to 释义：对……是未知的
* This new policy is unknown to me. 我不知道这个新政策。
* Because our product is unknown to consumers, our sales numbers are lacking. 由于客户不知道我们的产品，所以我们的产品销量不高。

## break into the market 释义：进入市场
* We’re hoping the new marketing strategy we talked about will allow us to break into the European market. 我希望我们之前谈论过的新市场策略可以帮助我们打开欧洲市场。
* It takes years of work to break into a new market. 打开一个新的市场需要好几年的努力。

## marketing objectives 释义：市场目标
* These marketing objectives are unable to be fulfilled. 这些市场目标是实现不了的。
* Our marketing objective is 70 percent of the Chinese market. 我们的市场目标是七成的中国市场。

## competitive advantage 释义：竞争优势
* Our low shipping costs give us a competitive advantage. 我们超低的运输成本是我们的竞争优势。
* Apple’s competitive advantage is their brand awareness. 苹果的品牌认知是他们的竞争优势。

## That would be doable 释义：这个是可执行的
* That would be doable if we got the resources we needed. 如果我们得到了我们想要的资源，这件事便可行得通。
* That would be doable if the boss said yes. 要是老板同意了，这个就能做。

## be of importance to 释义：对……很重要
* This outcome will be of importance to our future plans. 这个结果对未来的计划很重要。
* Our entrance into the Chinese market is of great importance to our stockholders. 进入中国市场对于我们的股东来说很重要。

## break the mold 释义：打破常规
* We’re trying to break the mold of real time strategy games with our new design. 我们正在尝试运用我们的新设计来打破实施战略游戏的老套路。
* He broke the mold with his new style of rock and roll. 他用自己全新的风格打破了摇滚本来的束缚。

## market research  释义：市场调查
* We need to increase our market research before we break into the market. 在进入市场之前，我们要加大市场调查的力度。
* I want to see the market research on this before I make a decision. 我需要先看一下市场调查然后再做决定。
