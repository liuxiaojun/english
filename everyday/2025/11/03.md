## What time is the most convenient 释义：什么时候对你来说最方便……
* What time is the most convenient for giving the presentation? Any time is ok by me.
你什么时候方便做报告呢？我随时都可以。
* What time is most convenient for you to come and discuss this issue?
您什么时候方便来讨论一下这个问题呢？

## Would be acceptable? 释义：……能行吗？
* Would it be acceptable if we showed up later in the evening? 我们晚上晚点到可以吗？
* Would it be acceptable if I took this sample with me to show my boss? 我拿着这个样品去见我的老板可行吗？

## work out the solution 释义：制定出解决方法
* I was able to work out the solution to this issue by myself. 我自己就能想出这个问题的解决方案。
* If you can’t work out the solution by yourself, ask IT to give you a hand. 如果你自己搞不定这个问题，你可以向IT人员求助。

## be fully booked 释义：（日程）排满了
* I’m fully booked this week that I couldn’t possibly take on anymore work. 这周我忙的焦头烂额，我根本不可能去干别的。
* The manager is fully booked today, so we have to postpone our meeting. 经理今天太忙了，所以我们只能推迟会议了。

## to see if 释义：看看是否
* I want you to come and to see if you’d be a good fit for the position. 我想让你来看看这个岗位是否合适你。
* To see if this will work out, we’re going to try it in the Australian market first. 我们将在澳洲市场先试试这个是否行得通。

## consultant 释义：n.咨询师
* I got a job as a consultant for Facebook and was able to work at home. 我得到一份Facebook咨询师的工作，并且可以在家上班。
* The fees for the consultant here are too high for us to bring him on. 咨询的费用太贵了，所以我们不能带他一起。

## high staff turnover 释义：高人员流动
* We have had high staff turnover since the company moved offices. 公司搬家了以后，我们的人员流动很大。
* We’ve been trying to lower the high staff turnover by increasing benefits. 我们一直尝试着提高员工福利来降低人员的流动。

## weigh 释义：v.权衡
* I’ll need to weigh both options a bit before I make a final decision. 在做最终决定之前，我需要权衡这两个选项。
* He weighed his options, and finally chose what he originally wanted to do. 在一番深思熟虑之后，他最终还是选择了一直以来想要做的事。
