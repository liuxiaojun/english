## May I take this opportunity to 释义：我可以借此机会……
* May I take this opportunity to talk to you about getting a mortgage for your house? 我能借此机会和你聊聊有关于房贷的事吗？
* May I take this opportunity to mention we've got a buy one get one offer currently running on all products over 10 dollars? 我可以借此机会重申一下，我们现在对十美金以下的产品都是买一送一的事吗？

## Would it be possible 释义：有可能……吗？
* Would it be possible to continue this conversation after lunch? 可以吃完午饭再说这个事吗？
* Would it be possible to ship these to us by next week? 有没有可能下周前把这些东西寄给我们？

## rearrange one's schedule 释义：重新安排某人的日程表
* He rearranged his schedule to come and visit our factory. 他重新安排了他的日程表来参观我们的工厂。
* We'd like to rearrange our schedule in order to fit another meeting in this afternoon. 我们想要重新安排一下日常表以便顺利进行下午的会议。

## take a rain check 释义：改期
* I'm going to take a rain check on that dinner at the Spanish place! 我打算改期去那家西班牙餐厅吃晚餐了。
* You can take a rain check on my offer to go out for coffee if you're busy this week. 要是你忙的话，我们可以改天再一起喝咖啡。

## stop by 释义：顺便访问
* If you have a chance you should stop by and visit our new headquarters. 要是你有机会，你真应该去看看咱们的新总部。
* She stopped by last week to hand in the rest of her paperwork. 她上周路过时把剩下的文稿交了。

## move into 释义：搬进
* We moved into our new home last year, and I have to say we love it! 我们去年搬进了新家，我不得不说我非常爱这里。
* We're going to be moving into the 4th floor of this building. 我们将要搬进这个大楼的四层了。

## a previous commitment 释义：提前有约
* I'd love to stop by Friday, but I've got a previous commitment that evening, so I'll have to take a rain check. 我是乐意周五过去，但是那天晚上我已经有约了，所以我不得不改期了。
* I've got a previous commitment that I just have to attend, I'm sorry. 我已有约，而且必须要去，很抱歉。

## fit something in one's schedule 释义：把某事和某人的日程吻合
* Honey, I'll be able to fit dinner with you in my schedule today. 亲爱的，今晚我可以和你一起吃饭啦。
* I wasn't able to fit your meeting with the CEO in his schedule. 我不能把你的会议安排到CEO的日程表中。
