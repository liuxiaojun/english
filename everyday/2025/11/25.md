## be covered in 释义：被包含
* What's going to be covered in the meeting? 会议包括什么内容？
* The agenda will tell us what will be covered in the meeting. 会议议程会告诉我们会议上有什么内容。

## corporate culture 释义：企业文化
* How is the corporate culture at your new job? 你新公司的企业文化怎么样？
* The corporate culture at Google is to treat its workers very well so most of them won't leave. 谷歌公司的企业文化是友好地对待员工，所以他们离职率低。

## make efforts to 释义：努力
* Let's make efforts to improve our performance in this area. 让我们一起努力提高这个区域的业绩吧！
* We didn't make efforts to improve ourselves, so our numbers suffered. 我们没有努力进行自我提升，所以我们的销售业绩受影响了。

## prepare the ground for 释义：为……做准备；为……打下基础
* We've been preparing the ground for this project for months. 我们已经为这个项目准备了数月了。
* He prepared the ground for the meeting weeks in advance. 他几周前就为会议做了准备。

## achieve one's potential 释义：发挥某人的潜力
* I really think I could achieve my true potential at this job. 我真的认为我能在这份工作上发挥我真正的潜力。
* A job that allows you to achieve your full potential is always attractive. 一个能够让你充分发挥个人潜力的工作是很有吸引力的。

## orientation 
* What'll be covered in this orientation? 入职培训包含什么内容？
* The agenda lists the topics in this orientation. 议程里有入职培训的所有主题。

## benefit 释义：n.福利
* With the benefit of hindsight, I should have taken the job. 早知道是这样，我当初应该接受这个工作。
* The company's staff benefits are the best in the industry. 这家公司的员工福利是业界最好的。

## manual 释义：n.手册
* We'll hand out a work manual after the training. 培训过后我们会分发工作手册。
* The manual covers everything you need to know about taking a sick break. 手册里有你需要了解的有关请病假的所有内容。
