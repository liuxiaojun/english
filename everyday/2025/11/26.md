## some specifics 释义：一些细节
* I’d like to see some specifics on when this project will be finished. 我想知道关于这个项目完成时间的一些细节。
* What are some specifics of your product? 你的产品的具体细节是什么？

## a great time saver 释义：省时的事物
* This new work arrangement is a great time saver. 这个新的工作安排非常省时。
* Computer’s and digital tools have been a great time saver over the past 20 years for businesses. 电脑和数码工具在过去20年间对于企业而言是非常省时的。

## sum up 释义：总结
* Can you sum up the main points of what they said for me? 你可以总结一下他们对我说的内容的大意吗？
* To sum up, we need to do more with less, and do it faster if we want to compete! 总结一下， 如果想要竞争，我们需要在更短时间内做得更多，做得更快。

## customer loyalty 释义：客户忠诚
* He’s got no customer loyalty at all, whatever it cheapest is good for him.他没有客户忠诚的概念，便宜的东西在他看来就是好的。
* Our “customer loyalty cards” give discounts to customers that stick with our products. 我们的“客户忠诚卡”给忠实于我们产品的顾客折扣。

## enable sb. to do sth. 释义：使某人做某事
* This app enables you to find stores near you that are having a sale on things you like. 这个手机软件让你能够找到附近正在打折的你喜欢的商品。
* Good English will enable you to communicate with foreign clients more easily. 好的英文水平可以使你与外国客户交流更简单。

## application 释义：n.应用
* Taobao has a cell phone application that allows you to shop using your phone. 淘宝有一个手机软件，你可以用它来购物。
* I think the Apple cell phone applications are better than the Microsoft ones. 我认为苹果的手机软件比微软的好。

## competitive 释义：adj. 竞争的
* These prices are too high and just aren’t competitive in our market. 这些价格太高了，在我们的市场上不具有竞争力。
* To stay competitive, a company always has to be improving and making new products. 为了保持竞争力，一个公司要保持进步，制造新产品。

## boost 释义：v.促进
* These changes will help to boost share prices. 这些变化将有助于提高股票价格。
* Economic theory suggests that the devalued pound will boost the economy. 经济学理论指出英镑贬值将会促进经济的发展。
