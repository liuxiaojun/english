## product demonstration 释义：产品演示
* I’m going to see Apple’s new product demonstration. 我将去参加苹果最新的产品演示会。
* A product demonstration is a good opportunity to impress future investors. 产品演示会是打动投资人的良机。

## collect comments on 释义：收集有关……的反馈
* We’ll be collecting comments on the new product demo afterwards. 我们将会在之后收集有关新产品会的反馈。
* His restaurant left a box for him to collect comments on the quality of the food. 他的餐厅给他留下了一个用来收集饭菜质量反馈的盒子。

## Will you honor us by ? 释义：可否赏脸来……?
* Would you honor us by trying out the new product? 可否请您试试我们的新产品呢？
* Would you honor us by coming to our end of year party? 可否邀请您来参加我们的年会呢？

## put off something 释义：推迟做某事
* If you put this off, you might never get around to doing it! 如果你推迟做这件事，你可能永远也不可能做它了。
* You’ve been putting him off for 3 months now, he knows what you’re doing. 你已经把他晾了三个月了，他很清楚你在干什么。

## later on 释义：过后
* He’ll be joining the group later on in the process. 他将会在一会儿后加入组织。
* I’d rather be sure now than sorry later on! 我宁可现在确定，也不愿将来后悔。

## a wise move 
* Investing in Uber was a wise move on his part. 投资Uber对他来说是一个明智之举。
* It wasn’t a wise move to let the only one person who understood the projects go. 只让一个人知道项目的进度不是一个明智之举。

## trial 释义：n.试用
* We’re going to have a trial for the new product before we show it to investors. 在我们向投资人展示产品之前，我们会先进行试用。
* The new car unfortunately failed its safety trials. 很遗憾，这款新车未通过它的安全测试。

## mess up  释义：搞乱
* He messed up so many times so he got fired! 他捅了太多次篓子，所以他被开除了。
* Proceed with care, or you'll mess up. 你必须小心行事，不然就要出乱子。
