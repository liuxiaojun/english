## develop 释义：v.发展；开发
* The company is spending 650 million dollars on developing new product. 公司投入6.5亿美元来开发新的产品。 
* I have gradually developed a new habit of setting timetables for my work. 我已经逐渐养成了在工作中做时间表的新习惯。

## profit 释义：n.利润；收益 
* Thanks to our hard work, our company got 5 percent change in profit last year. 由于我们的辛勤工作，我们公司去年获得利润率提升了5个百分点。
* It will be so hard for Asian Airline to make a profit this year. 今年亚洲航空公司很难实现盈利。

## fact 释义：n.事实
* We shouldn`t lie and hide the facts at an interview. 面试中我们不应该撒谎和隐瞒事实。
* Once I persuaded our partners with many facts and numbers. 我曾经用很多的实例和数据说服了我们的合作伙伴。

## project 释义：n.项目；课题
* The total cost of the project was more than 240 million dollars. 该项目的总成本超过了2.4亿美元。
* I took part in some projects related to my major when I was in the university. 大学期间我参加了一些和我专业相关的一些课题。

## mentor 释义：n.（无经验之人的）有经验可信赖的指导者
* I am honored to be able to call him a friend and a mentor. 我非常荣幸地能有他这样的朋友和导师。
* I played the role of a mentor for many new-comers at my previous job. 在过去那份工作中，我曾多次充当了新员工的指导顾问。
